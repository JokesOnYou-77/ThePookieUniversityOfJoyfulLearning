Welcome to Pookie University Admissions

At Pookie University, we believe in nurturing creativity and fostering friendships. Join us for an unforgettable educational journey!

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Personal statement (250-500 words)
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Portfolio (for design-related programs)

Application Process

  1. Fill out the online application form on our website.
  2. Submit all required documents, including your personal statement and letters of recommendation.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Attend an interview (if required for your program).
  5. Receive your admission decision via email.
